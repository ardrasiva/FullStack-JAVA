Important Notice

Dear students, we are delighted to announce that our school will be celebrating its Annual Day next Friday. The event will be a wonderful celebration of the talents, creativity, and achievements of our students. The program will include a variety of cultural performances such as music, dance, drama, and other exciting activities presented by students from different classes. All students are encouraged to actively participate in the celebrations and make the event memorable. Students who are interested in participating should register their names with their respective class teachers before the deadline. Rehearsals for the performances will be conducted during the coming week, and participants are requested to attend them regularly. Parents and guardians are warmly invited to attend the Annual Day celebration and encourage our talented students. The event will also include an award ceremony to recognize students who have achieved excellence in academics, sports, arts, and extracurricular activities throughout the academic year. Further details regarding the event schedule, timings, dress code, and other arrangements will be announced by the school authorities soon. We look forward to the enthusiastic participation and cooperation of all students, teachers, and parents in making this year's Annual Day celebration a grand success.
